CAPABILITIES

Optical Printing Services

01

Freeform & Aspheric Lenses

Non-rotationally symmetric freeform optics printed via multi-material inkjet. Corrects for off-axis aberrations in compact optical systems. Used in augmented reality waveguides, head-mounted displays, and automotive LIDAR systems.

02

Gradient-Index (GRIN) Optics

Refractive index gradient printed continuously through lens volume — no discrete layers. Planar GRIN elements performing the optical function of curved surfaces. Enables flat, thin lens systems for endoscopy and wearable imaging.

03

Micro-Lens Arrays

Regular and irregular micro-lens array production via nano-DLP. Lenslet diameters from 50µm to 5mm. Used for light field cameras, solar concentrators, beam homogenization, and display diffusion.

OPTICAL MATERIALS

Printable Optical Polymers

Optical performance begins with material selection — refractive index, Abbe number, and transmission bandwidth define the lens specification.

  • ● NOA61 (n=1.56) — general purpose UV-cure optic
  • ● High-RI polymer (n=1.65) — compact lens design
  • ● PMMA-equivalent — broadband visible transmission
  • ● Silicone optical grade — soft contact and wearable optics
  • ● Gradient-index photopolymer — GRIN element production
  • ● Glass-like optical ceramic — thermally stable IR optics
